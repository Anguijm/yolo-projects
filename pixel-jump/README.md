# Pixel Jump

Canvas platformer with proper game feel. Variable-height jump, coyote time, jump buffering, AABB collision, 5 hand-crafted levels with coins, spikes, and exit goals. Touch controls for mobile.

## Features

- Custom physics engine: gravity, friction, terminal velocity
- Variable-height jump (release early = lower jump)
- Coyote time (jump a few frames after walking off a ledge)
- Jump buffering (press jump slightly before landing)
- AABB collision against tile-based levels
- 5 hand-crafted levels with escalating difficulty
- Coins to collect, spikes to avoid, exit portal to reach
- Touch controls: left/right zones + jump zone (bottom of screen)
- Keyboard: Arrow keys/WASD + Space
- Particle effects on jump, land, coin collect, and death
- Camera following with level bounds clamping
- Minimal geometric art style on dark background

## How to Run

Open `index.html`. Use arrow keys or touch the bottom of the screen to move and jump. Collect coins, avoid spikes, reach the green exit. 5 levels of increasing difficulty.
