# Orbit Well

Gravity well particle sandbox. Click anywhere to place mass points and watch particles orbit, spiral, and get absorbed. Click on an existing well to remove it. Right-click also removes nearest well on desktop.

## Features

- Particle streams spawn continuously from screen edges
- Click to place gravity wells with configurable mass
- Click on a well to remove it (mobile-friendly), right-click on desktop
- Particles absorbed when too close to wells
- Speed-based HSL coloring (blue → cyan → white)
- Fading trail effect via semi-transparent overlay
- Burst button spawns 200 particles from center
- Mass and spawn rate sliders
- OLED black, mobile-first, all PWA metas

## How to Run

Open `index.html`. Click to place gravity wells. Watch particles orbit and spiral. Adjust mass and spawn rate with sliders. Hit Burst for a particle explosion.
