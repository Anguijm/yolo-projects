# Iso City

Isometric city builder with strategy mechanics. Place roads, houses, shops, and parks on a 12x12 grid. Buildings level up based on adjacency — houses near parks grow, shops near roads earn more. Manage your budget to maximize population and score.

## Features

- Isometric 3D rendering with procedural buildings (no sprites, pure canvas paths)
- 4 building types: Road ($10), House ($50), Shop ($80), Park ($40)
- Adjacency bonuses: houses level up near parks, shops near roads
- Combined road+park adjacency unlocks max level buildings
- Revenue generated by shops every 2 seconds
- Population from houses contributes to score
- Pan camera by dragging, tap to build
- Bulldoze tool to clear tiles
- Floating text feedback for costs and errors
- Proper isometric z-sorting (painter's algorithm by depth)
- Keyboard shortcuts: 1-5 for tool selection
- OLED dark theme, mobile-first

## How to Run

Open `index.html`. Select a building type from the toolbar, tap the grid to place it. Pan by dragging. Build roads next to shops for revenue, parks next to houses for population. Maximize your score!
